# Water Quality and Aquaculture Use Case

This use case demonstrates how MBT55 improves water quality in  
irrigation systems, rivers, ponds, and aquaculture environments,  
converting these improvements into PBPE credits.  
Producers gain financial value while restoring aquatic ecosystems.

---

# 1. Overview

MBT55 improves:

- Water clarity  
- Organic load reduction  
- Ammonia and nitrate balance  
- Pathogen suppression  
- Oxygen stability  
- Algal bloom prevention  

These improvements generate PBPE credits.

---

# 2. PBPE Credits Generated

Water systems produce:

- **WQC**（Water Quality Credit）  
- **DRC**（Disease Reduction Credit for aquatic pathogens）  
- **AMRC**（AMR Reduction Credit）  

---

# 3. Data Inputs

Required data:

- Baseline water quality  
- Ammonia / nitrate levels  
- Dissolved oxygen  
- Pathogen load  
- Algal bloom frequency  
- Sediment organic matter  
- Aquaculture mortality rates  

---

# 4. Workflow

1. **MBT55 applied to water system**  
2. **Organic load decreases**  
3. **Pathogens suppressed**  
4. **Water clarity improves**  
5. **Impact data collected**  
6. **PBPE credits issued**  
7. **Credits sold to ESG buyers**  

---

# 5. Financial Impact

Producers gain:

- Higher survival rates  
- Faster growth  
- Reduced disease outbreaks  
- Lower water treatment costs  
- PBPE credit revenue  

---

# 6. Example Scenario

A 10-hectare aquaculture pond:

- Ammonia reduced by 34%  
- Pathogen load reduced by 29%  
- Survival rate increased by 12%  

Credits issued:

- 88 WQC units  
- 22 DRC units  

---

# 7. One-Sentence Summary

**PBPE converts water quality improvements and disease reduction into  
financial credits, enabling sustainable aquaculture and clean water systems.**
