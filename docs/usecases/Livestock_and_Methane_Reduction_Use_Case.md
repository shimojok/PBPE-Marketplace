# Livestock and Methane Reduction Use Case

This use case demonstrates how MBT55 reduces methane emissions in  
livestock systems and converts these reductions into PBPE credits.  
Farmers gain new revenue streams while improving animal health and  
reducing climate impact.

---

# 1. Overview

MBT55 improves:

- Gut microbiome stability  
- SCFA production  
- Feed efficiency  
- Immune activation  
- Methane suppression  

These improvements generate PBPE credits.

---

# 2. PBPE Credits Generated

Livestock systems produce:

- **MRC**（Methane Reduction Credit）  
- **LHC**（Livestock Health Credit）  
- **FEC**（Feed Efficiency Credit）  
- **AMRC**（AMR Reduction Credit）  

---

# 3. Data Inputs

Required data:

- Baseline methane emissions  
- Feed intake  
- Weight gain  
- Health metrics  
- Manure emissions  
- Respiration chamber data  
- Sensor logs  

---

# 4. Workflow

1. **MBT55 added to feed or water**  
2. **Methane emissions decrease**  
3. **Feed efficiency improves**  
4. **Health metrics increase**  
5. **Impact data collected**  
6. **PBPE credits issued**  
7. **Credits sold on marketplace**  

---

# 5. Financial Impact

Farmers gain:

- Methane credit revenue  
- Feed cost savings  
- Reduced veterinary costs  
- Improved weight gain  
- Lower mortality  

---

# 6. Example Scenario

A 200-head dairy farm:

- Methane reduced by 18%  
- Feed efficiency improved by 7%  
- Somatic cell count reduced by 22%  

Credits issued:

- 412 tCO₂e MRC  
- 200 LHC units  
- 14 FEC units  

---

# 7. One-Sentence Summary

**PBPE converts livestock methane reduction and health improvements  
into financial credits, enabling climate-positive animal agriculture.**
