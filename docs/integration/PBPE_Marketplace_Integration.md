# PBPE Marketplace Integration

The PBPE Marketplace enables the buying, selling, and trading of PBPE credits  
generated from MBT55-driven biological and climate improvements.  
It connects farmers, enterprises, governments, and ESG buyers in a unified  
biosecurity and climate finance ecosystem.

---

# 1. Marketplace Architecture

The marketplace consists of:

- Credit registry  
- Order book  
- Matching engine  
- Wallet system  
- Smart contract layer  
- Dashboard interface  

---

# 2. Supported Credit Types

Tradable credits include:

- ΔC‑SCC（Soil Carbon Credit）  
- MRC（Methane Reduction Credit）  
- NRC（Nitrous Oxide Reduction Credit）  
- SRC（Spoilage Reduction Credit）  
- QPC（Quality Premium Credit）  
- DRC（Disease Reduction Credit）  
- LHC（Livestock Health Credit）  
- FEC（Feed Efficiency Credit）  
- AMRC（AMR Reduction Credit）  
- WQC（Water Quality Credit）  

---

# 3. Marketplace Operations

Supported operations:

- List credits for sale  
- Place buy orders  
- Match trades  
- Transfer credits  
- Set price floors  
- Generate invoices  
- Export transaction logs  

---

# 4. Smart Contract Integration

Each trade triggers:

- Ownership transfer  
- Contract signature  
- Registry update  
- Audit log creation  

Contracts are:

- Immutable  
- Transparent  
- Gas-efficient  
- Verifiable  

---

# 5. Price Discovery

Marketplace pricing is influenced by:

- Supply and demand  
- ESG buyer interest  
- Government incentives  
- PBPE Price Stability Index  
- Regional climate policies  

---

# 6. Enterprise Integration

Enterprises can:

- Offset emissions  
- Improve supply chain sustainability  
- Purchase quality premiums  
- Support regenerative agriculture  
- Meet ESG reporting requirements  

---

# 7. One-Sentence Summary

**The PBPE Marketplace enables transparent, secure trading of PBPE credits,  
connecting biological regeneration with global climate and ESG finance.**
