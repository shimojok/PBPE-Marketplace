# PBPE Market Simulation Model

The PBPE Market Simulation Model provides a quantitative framework  
for forecasting credit supply, demand, pricing, volatility, and  
market stability under different scenarios.  
It supports policy design, investor analysis, and marketplace planning.

---

# 1. Overview

The simulation model evaluates:

- Credit supply growth  
- Buyer demand curves  
- Price elasticity  
- Volatility patterns  
- Insurance pool solvency  
- Regional adoption rates  
- Climate and biological shocks  

---

# 2. Simulation Inputs

- Historical credit issuance  
- Biological impact data  
- Climate variability indices  
- Enterprise procurement trends  
- Government policy incentives  
- Market liquidity  
- Verification throughput  

---

# 3. Simulation Engine Components

## 3.1 Supply Model
Predicts credit issuance based on:

- MBT55 adoption  
- Biological impact  
- Seasonal cycles  
- Regional factors  

## 3.2 Demand Model
Predicts buyer interest based on:

- ESG budgets  
- Regulatory pressure  
- Price sensitivity  
- Credit ratings  

## 3.3 Price Model
Uses:

- Intrinsic value  
- Market demand index  
- Volatility index  
- Risk adjustment factor  

## 3.4 Volatility Model
Includes:

- GARCH-like volatility  
- Seasonal variance  
- Shock events  

## 3.5 Insurance Solvency Model
Simulates:

- Payout frequency  
- Pool balance  
- Reinsurance triggers
