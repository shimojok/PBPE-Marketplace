# PBPE Pricing and Market Valuation Model

The PBPE Pricing and Market Valuation Model defines how PBPE credits  
are priced, how market value is determined, and how price stability  
is maintained across the PBPE Marketplace.

---

# 1. Overview

PBPE pricing is based on:

- Scientific impact value  
- Market demand  
- Verification level  
- Regional factors  
- Credit scarcity  
- Policy incentives  

PBPE uses a hybrid pricing model combining intrinsic value and market dynamics.

---

# 2. Intrinsic Value Calculation

Intrinsic value is determined by:

Intrinsic_Value = Impact_Value × Social_Cost_of_Carbon × Verification_Weight × Regional_Adjustment


Where:

- **Impact_Value** = tCO₂e or equivalent units  
- **Verification_Weight** = 0.85–1.15  
- **Regional_Adjustment** accounts for local climate policy  

---

# 3. Market-Based Pricing

Market pricing is influenced by:

- Supply and demand  
- ESG buyer interest  
- Government incentives  
- Seasonal biological cycles  
- Credit type scarcity  
- Marketplace liquidity  

---

# 4. Price Stability Mechanisms

PBPE uses:

- Price floors  
- Dynamic ceilings  
- Circuit breakers  
- Market maker incentives  
- Auction-based resets  

These prevent manipulation and excessive volatility.

---

# 5. Credit Rating System (A–D)

PBPE assigns ratings based on:

- Data quality  
- Verification level  
- Methodology robustness  
- Regional risk  
- Historical performance  

Ratings influence price multipliers:

A = ×1.20 B = ×1.00 C = ×0.85 D = ×0.60


---

# 6. Marketplace Valuation Model

Total PBPE market value:

Market_Value = Σ (Credit_Price × Credit_Volume)

Segmented by:

- Soil carbon  
- Methane  
- Nitrous oxide  
- Spoilage  
- Water quality  
- Disease reduction  

---

# 7. Example Scenario

A batch of MRC credits:

- Impact: 412 tCO₂e  
- Intrinsic value: $38/tCO₂e  
- Rating: A (×1.20)  
- Market demand index: 1.14  

Final price:

Price = 38 × 1.20 × 1.14 = $52.0 per credit

---

# 8. One-Sentence Summary

**PBPE pricing combines intrinsic scientific value with market dynamics  
to create stable, transparent, and fair valuation for all PBPE credits.**
