# PBPE Governance Model

The PBPE Governance Model defines the institutional structure,  
decision-making processes, and accountability mechanisms that ensure  
the integrity, transparency, and long-term stability of the PBPE Marketplace.

---

# 1. Governance Principles

PBPE governance is based on:

- Transparency  
- Scientific integrity  
- Multi-stakeholder participation  
- Decentralized verification  
- Fairness and inclusivity  
- Anti-manipulation safeguards  

---

# 2. Governance Bodies

PBPE governance consists of five core bodies:

## 2.1 Steering Committee
Responsible for:

- Strategic direction  
- Policy approval  
- Oversight of marketplace integrity  

Members include scientists, policymakers, and industry leaders.

## 2.2 Technical Advisory Board
Responsible for:

- Methodology updates  
- Scientific validation  
- Data quality standards  

Includes experts in soil science, microbiology, climate science, and AI.

## 2.3 Verifier Council
Responsible for:

- Accreditation of verifiers  
- Verification rule enforcement  
- Dispute mediation  

## 2.4 Farmer & Producer Assembly
Ensures representation of:

- Smallholder farmers  
- Large-scale producers  
- Livestock operators  
- Aquaculture producers  

## 2.5 Enterprise & ESG Stakeholder Panel
Represents:

- Food companies  
- Retailers  
- Financial institutions  
- ESG buyers  

---

# 3. Governance Processes

PBPE uses structured processes for:

- Methodology updates  
- Verification rule changes  
- Marketplace parameter adjustments  
- Smart contract upgrades  
- Dispute resolution  
- Emergency actions  

All changes require multi-party approval.

---

# 4. Voting System

PBPE uses:

- Weighted voting by stakeholder group  
- Multi-signature approval for major changes  
- Transparent vote logs  
- Public governance reports  

---

# 5. Transparency Mechanisms

PBPE ensures transparency through:

- Public registry access  
- Open methodology documentation  
- Verifier accreditation lists  
- Governance meeting minutes  
- Smart contract audit reports  

---

# 6. One-Sentence Summary

**The PBPE Governance Model ensures transparent, science-based,  
multi-stakeholder oversight of the PBPE Marketplace.**
