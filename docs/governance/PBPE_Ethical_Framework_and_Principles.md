# PBPE Ethical Framework and Principles

The PBPE Ethical Framework ensures that PBPE credits, methodologies,  
and marketplace operations uphold fairness, equity, transparency,  
and responsible use of biological and environmental data.

---

# 1. Ethical Principles

PBPE is guided by:

- Fairness  
- Inclusivity  
- Transparency  
- Scientific integrity  
- Environmental responsibility  
- Data privacy and protection  
- Anti-exploitation safeguards  

---

# 2. Fairness and Equity

PBPE ensures:

- Equal access for smallholder farmers  
- Fair compensation for biological improvements  
- No exploitation of vulnerable communities  
- Transparent pricing mechanisms  

PBPE promotes equitable climate finance.

---

# 3. Data Ethics

PBPE enforces:

- Informed consent for data collection  
- Privacy protection  
- Anonymization of sensitive data  
- Secure storage and encryption  
- Ethical use of AI and analytics  

Data is never used without authorization.

---

# 4. Environmental Ethics

PBPE ensures:

- No harmful biological interventions  
- No ecological disruption  
- Support for regenerative practices  
- Long-term ecosystem health  

MBT55 and PBPE methodologies prioritize ecological safety.

---

# 5. Governance Ethics

PBPE governance includes:

- Multi-stakeholder representation  
- Transparent decision-making  
- Public reporting  
- Anti-corruption safeguards  
- Conflict-of-interest policies  

Governance decisions are accountable and traceable.

---

# 6. Ethical Use of Credits

PBPE prohibits:

- Double counting  
- Fraudulent credit creation  
- Manipulation of biological data  
- Misuse of credits for greenwashing  

Credits must reflect real, measurable impact.

---

# 7. One-Sentence Summary

**The PBPE Ethical Framework ensures fairness, transparency, ecological  
responsibility, and ethical data use across the entire PBPE ecosystem.**
