# **PBPE‑Biosecurity Dashboard**

A unified interface for global biosecurity, food security, and climate metrics.

---

## **1. Global Biosecurity Overview**

**Indicators:**

- Global crop disease hotspots
- FAO Food Price Index
- Livestock epidemic alerts
- Global food loss (pre‑ and post‑harvest)
- Climate stress indicators (drought, heat, rainfall anomalies)

**Purpose:**  
Provide a **global situational awareness layer** for foundations and governments.

---

## **2. Crop Disease Risk & MBT55 Impact**

**For each crop:**

- Disease incidence
- Yield loss
- MBT55 intervention
- Post‑intervention improvement
- Price stability impact

**Visuals:**

- Before/After bar charts
- Disease risk heatmaps
- Yield improvement curves

Explore: **Disease Matrix**

---

## **3. Livestock Biosecurity (One Health)**

**Indicators:**

- Infection rate
- Culling numbers
- Antibiotic usage
- Gut microbiome stability
- CH₄ reduction from manure
- 24‑hour decomposition rate

**Visuals:**

- Infection suppression curves
- Antibiotic reduction charts
- Methane reduction graphs

---

## **4. Post‑Harvest Loss & Food Price Stability**

**Indicators:**

- Spoilage rate (e.g., 40% → <5%)
- Shelf‑life extension
- Food loss reduction
- Market price volatility
- PBPE Price Stability Index

**Visuals:**

- Spoilage Before/After
- Price volatility compression
- Food loss reduction curves

---

## **5. Soil–Microbiome–Climate Engine**

**Indicators:**

- Soil microbiome diversity
- SCFA production
- Fulvic acid concentration
- Soil carbon (SOC)
- ΔC (carbon sequestration)
- CH₄ and N₂O reduction

**Visuals:**

- Hypercycle diagram
- SOC increase curves
- GHG reduction charts
