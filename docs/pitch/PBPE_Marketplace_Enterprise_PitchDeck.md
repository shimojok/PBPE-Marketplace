# PBPE Marketplace – Investor & Enterprise Pitch (Outline)

## Slide 1 – Title
- PBPE Marketplace  
- Turning Biosecurity and Regeneration into Financial Assets

## Slide 2 – Problem
- Agriculture, food systems, and climate are fragmented.
- ESG and Scope 3 reporting lack **verified, biological grounding**.
- Biosecurity, food loss, and quality are treated as “costs”, not “assets”.

## Slide 3 – Solution
- PBPE OS + PBPE Marketplace:
  - Convert MBT55-driven improvements into **PBPE Credits**.
  - Provide **API-based access** to verified impact.
  - Enable **trading and portfolio management** of biosecurity and climate assets.

## Slide 4 – How It Works (Layers)
- Layer 1: MBT-Biosecurity-Engine  
- Layer 2: PBPE Dashboard (KPIs & PBPE Value)  
- Layer 3: PBPE Finance (Credits)  
- Layer 4: PBPE Marketplace (Trading & API)  
- Layer 5: Reporting (ESG & Scope 3)

## Slide 5 – Evidence (MBT55)
- Disease suppression (Shiitake, poultry, bees)
- Growth enhancement (eggplant, cabbage, strawberry, carrot)
- Environmental regeneration (Iwanuma sludge & salt)
- These are **not simulations** – they are **physical evidence**.

## Slide 6 – PBPE Credits
- Biosecurity Credits  
- Carbon Credits  
- Food Loss Credits  
- Quality Credits  
- Stability Credits  
- Each backed by:
  - KPIs
  - MBT55 evidence
  - Marketplace verification

## Slide 7 – Enterprise Workflow
1. Run PBPE Dashboard v3  
2. Generate KPIs  
3. Call PBPE Marketplace API  
4. Receive credit prices & impact  
5. Purchase credits  
6. Export ESG / Scope 3 reports

## Slide 8 – Business Model
- Credit transaction fees  
- Enterprise subscription (API access)  
- White-label ESG reporting  
- Data services (benchmarking, risk scoring)

## Slide 9 – Competitive Advantage
- Real biological engine (MBT55), not just models  
- Full stack: biology → KPIs → credits → marketplace → ESG  
- Evidence-backed, auditable, and API-native

## Slide 10 – Vision
- A planetary operating system where:
  - Regeneration is **measured**  
  - Biosecurity is **priced**  
  - Climate action is **tradeable**  
  - Agriculture becomes a **financial-grade infrastructure**
