# PBPE Credits Specification  
Unified Credit Framework for the PBPE Marketplace  
Version 1.0 – English Edition

The PBPE Credit System converts biological, environmental, and economic improvements generated by MBT55 and PBPE-aligned practices into **standardized, tradeable digital credits**.

These credits are used across:
- PBPE Dashboard (Layer 2)
- PBPE Finance (Layer 3)
- PBPE Marketplace (Layer 4)

---

# 1. Overview of PBPE Credits

PBPE Credits quantify improvements in:
- Biosecurity  
- Carbon & GHG  
- Food Loss Reduction  
- Quality Enhancement  
- Price Stability  

Each credit type is:
- **Measurable** (derived from KPIs)  
- **Verifiable** (supported by MBT55 evidence)  
- **Tradeable** (via PBPE Marketplace API)  
- **Interoperable** (usable in ESG, Scope 3, and financial reporting)  

---

# 2. Credit Types

## 2.1 Biosecurity Credits (BSC)
**Definition:**  
Credits awarded for reductions in disease pressure, pathogen load, odor emissions, and microbial risk.

**Formula:**  

BSC = (Disease_Reduction_% × Area_ha)  
+ (Odor_Reduction_Index × Livestock_Units)


**Supported by Evidence:**  
- Mold suppression (Shiitake)  
- Odor elimination (Poultry)  
- Chalkbrood prevention (Beekeeping)  
- Sludge sanitation (Iwanuma)

---

## 2.2 Carbon Credits (PCC – PBPE Carbon Credits)
**Definition:**  
Credits awarded for reductions in CO₂e emissions and increases in soil carbon (ΔC).

**Formula:**  

PCC = ΔC_tC + GHG_Reduction_tCO2e


**Supported by Evidence:**  
- 24-hour fermentation (reduced CO₂ loss)  
- Soil carbon recovery (Iwanuma)  

---

## 2.3 Food Loss Credits (FLC)
**Definition:**  
Credits awarded for reductions in spoilage, mold, post-harvest loss, and mortality.

**Formula:**  

FLC = (Baseline_Loss_t – MBT55_Loss_t)


**Supported by Evidence:**  
- Mold prevention (Shiitake)  
- Reduced larval mortality (Beekeeping)  
- Reduced fruit rot (Strawberry)

---

## 2.4 Quality Credits (QC)
**Definition:**  
Credits awarded for measurable improvements in product quality.

**Quality Indicators:**  
- Sweetness (Brix)  
- Firmness  
- Coloration (e.g., Red Sea Bream naturalization)  
- Uniformity  
- Nutrient density  

**Formula:**  

QC = Quality_Score × Yield_t


---

## 2.5 Price Stability Credits (PSC)
**Definition:**  
Credits awarded for reducing volatility in production and market risk.

**Formula:**  

PSC = Stability_Index × Output_Value_USD


**Supported by Evidence:**  
- Odor reduction → stable livestock performance  
- Stress tolerance → stable crop yields  

---

# 3. Credit Issuance Workflow

1. MBT55 or PBPE-aligned intervention applied  
2. KPIs measured via PBPE Dashboard  
3. Credits calculated using PBPE Engine  
4. Credits verified (evidence + data)  
5. Credits minted on PBPE Marketplace  
6. Credits become tradeable assets  

---

# 4. API Integration (Marketplace Layer)

Credits are accessible via:

- `GET /credits/price`  
- `POST /credits/buy`  
- `POST /credits/sell`  
- `GET /credits/portfolio`  
- `GET /impact/scope3`
