# 🌱 PBPE‑Biosecurity Credits (English Version)

PBPE‑Biosecurity Credits are **quantified outputs** generated from  
MBT55 Biosecurity interventions across agriculture, livestock, food systems,  
soil, climate, and One Health.

These credits represent **measurable, verifiable, and monetizable outcomes**  
within the PBPE Marketplace.

---

# 1. What Are PBPE‑Biosecurity Credits?

PBPE‑Biosecurity Credits are units of value created from:

- Disease loss reduction  
- Spoilage reduction  
- Yield stabilization  
- Quality improvement  
- Soil carbon gain (ΔC)  
- CH₄ reduction  
- N₂O reduction  
- Antibiotic reduction  
- One Health improvements  

Each credit corresponds to a **verified improvement** in biosecurity performance.

---

# 2. PBPE‑Biosecurity Credit Categories

PBPE‑Biosecurity Credits │ ├── 1. Disease Reduction Credits (DRC) ├── 2. Spoilage Reduction Credits (SRC) ├── 3. Yield Stability Credits (YSC) ├── 4. Quality Premium Credits (QPC) ├── 5. Soil Carbon Credits (ΔC‑SCC) ├── 6. Methane Reduction Credits (MRC) ├── 7. Nitrous Oxide Reduction Credits (NRC) ├── 8. Antibiotic Reduction Credits (ARC) └── 9. One Health Credits (OHC)


Each credit type is tied to a **specific measurable KPI**.

---

# 3. Measurement Framework

### **1. Disease Reduction Credits (DRC)**
- Formula:  
  `DRC = (Baseline Loss – Post‑MBT55 Loss) × Crop Value`

### **2. Spoilage Reduction Credits (SRC)**
- Example:  
  Watermelon spoilage **40% → <5%**

### **3. Yield Stability Credits (YSC)**
- Based on Yield Stability Index (YSI)

### **4. Quality Premium Credits (QPC)**
- Based on quality metrics (Brix, aroma, bean grade, etc.)

### **5. Soil Carbon Credits (ΔC‑SCC)**
- Based on SOC increase and humic substance formation

### **6. Methane Reduction Credits (MRC)**
- Based on CH₄ flux reduction from soil and manure

### **7. Nitrous Oxide Reduction Credits (NRC)**
- Based on N₂O emission suppression

### **8. Antibiotic Reduction Credits (ARC)**
- Based on reduced antibiotic usage in livestock

### **9. One Health Credits (OHC)**
- Based on AMR reduction  
- Gut microbiome improvement  
- Environmental pathogen load reduction
