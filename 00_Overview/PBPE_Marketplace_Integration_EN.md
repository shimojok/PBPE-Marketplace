# 🌐 PBPE Marketplace Integration (English Version)

A unified architecture connecting **MBT55 Biosecurity**,  
**Agriculture**, **Livestock**, **Food Systems**, **Soil**, **Climate**,  
and **One Health** into a single economic and ecological platform.

PBPE = **Planetary Bio‑Positive Economy**  
A system that converts biological improvements into **economic value**,  
**risk reduction**, and **climate credits**.

---

# 1. PBPE Marketplace – Top‑Level Architecture

PBPE Marketplace │ ├── PBPE‑Coffee (Value Creation) ├── PBPE‑Biosecurity (Risk Reduction) ├── PBPE‑Finance (Climate Finance Engine) ├── PBPE‑Dashboard (Visualization Layer) └── PBPE‑Health (Future Module)


**PBPE‑Biosecurity** is the “risk‑reduction engine”  
that stabilizes production, reduces losses, and generates climate value.

---

# 2. PBPE‑Biosecurity Integration Structure

PBPE‑Biosecurity │ ├── Agriculture Biosecurity ├── Livestock Biosecurity ├── Food System Stability ├── Soil Regeneration ├── Climate Mitigation └── One Health Integration

Each module receives **MBT55 Biosecurity KPIs**  
and converts them into **economic and climate outputs**.

---

# 3. MBT55 → PBPE Value Conversion Flow

MBT55 Biosecurity Effects │ ├── Disease Loss Reduction ├── Spoilage Reduction ├── Yield Gain ├── Quality Premium ├── Cost Reduction (fertilizer, pesticide, antibiotics) ├── Soil Carbon Gain (ΔC) ├── CH₄ Reduction └── N₂O Reduction │ ▼ PBPE Value Engine │ ├── Economic Value (Yield × Quality × Stability) ├── Risk Reduction Value (Loss Reduction) ├── Climate Credits (ΔC, CH₄, N₂O) └── One Health Value

---

# 4. PBPE Marketplace Data Flow

MBT55 Field Data │ ▼ Biosecurity KPIs │ ▼ PBPE‑Biosecurity Module │ ▼ PBPE Value Engine │ ▼ PBPE Marketplace │ ├── PBPE‑Finance (Climate Credits) ├── PBPE‑Dashboard (Visualization) ├── PBPE‑Coffee (Value Creation) └── PBPE‑Health (Future)
